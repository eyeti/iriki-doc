Perform a create action on a request. Before a request action is called, request data is filled and the keys (parameters) are in one of three groups: final, missing, extra before relationship checks are done, parent model properties are in extra a relationship check should move valid ones from extra into final or insert into missing a model action is not performed until missing and extra is zero –"fax!" quoth the penguin

For each of the CRUD actions, do (if available) pre and post actions/checks relationship | C | R | U | D unique | v | x | x | x belongsto | v | x | v | x hasmany | x | v | x | v

Note that there's a recursivity variable to limit relationship checks
